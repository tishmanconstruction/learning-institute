What is BIM

Evoluation of coordination from pen/pencil to 2D CAD, to 3D Modeling (geometry without data), and BIM which is the combination of 3D geometry with embedded data. "Data-Rich"

What is BIM

Intelligent data assists the design/construction team when linked to MEP, structural, and architectural geometry. Information includes: Item, Type, Size, Quanitity and is linked to a model that is virtually "real".

What is BIM

Data-Rich models ca be used to establish quanitities, calculate cost, and develop a schedule. The ability to navigate in this virtual representation of the building allows team members to better understand problems leading to increased collaboration and problem solving.
